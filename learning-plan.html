Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

At present my focus is to move away from my career in Banking and to get a Job or enter into a Career as a Software Developer. The reason for this recent push is to develop a skill set that will have value and provide Job Security into the future. I also enjoy problem solving and am interested in technical developments.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Setting aside the time and patience to sit down and do the work. Staying focused and concentrating on the work at hand, avoiding distractions. I regularly go to the gym to keep myself strong and healthy. Knowing when to take breaks and celebrating wins. Not to get into too much of a negative space if something goes wrong and avoid beating myself up over failures. Taking breaks and reflecting on the work that has been done is a good attitude I have come to overtime.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Team skills such as inter communications skills and working within a team to achieve an outcome. Managing Team dynamics, knowing when to seek support for myself and when to support others. Interview and Job-seeking skills to get into the industry and through interview processes and personal brand development.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:

1. An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives

I have left my job, so as I can give my total attention to the program. I also have to note that I have completed this course previously. However, I am aware that the curriculum can change and the material covered may have matured. It also needs to be considered that a little bit of knowledge is a dangerous thing. If there are challenges that are overwhelming and I find that I am struggling, I feel it would be best to present this issue to a support team member or tutor and the earliest opportunity.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

Make a list of what the issues are and break them into sizable chunks. Remind myself to take a break to get some perspective. Focus on the large tasks first (big rock’s), break down the task into sizable tasks and focus on completing them. If I am very far behind then it is best to raise your hand and ask for help.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

Initially look to google and other resources then failing this look to group conversations on Discord. Still having issues I will re-read tutorials or tasks I am assigned and approach with a fresh perspective. Failing all of this I will raise my hand.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I understand that they are leading the cohort through the material, I do not expect that individuals leading the DevAcademy program to do the exercises for cohort members. However, I hope that I can infer or understand the material from their directions and be able to apply the techniques as assigned.
